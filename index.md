## About Anne Gentle

Anne is an industry-recognized author whose books promote collaboration among developers, writers, and other stakeholders within open-source and enterprise communities. Most recently, Anne is working at Pismo, a Visa company, as a technical writing manager in the developer experience team. Previously, she was a Director for Developer Experience at [Cisco](https://developer.cisco.com) DevNet, working on developer relations, API documentation quality, and tutorials.


Anne wrote the book, _Docs Like Code_, to share tested tools and techniques with all who want to provide helpful, accurate technical information to the world. Get a copy for yourself at [https://docslikecode.com/book](https://docslikecode.com/book). And if you're going to learn docs-as-code techniques using hands-on tutorials, read [https://docslikecode.com/learn](https://docslikecode.com/learn).
